Dr Tehri Microscopic Dental Clinic and Health Care Centre is one of India’s finest multi-speciality dental clinic located in the capital city of Haryana. It is located in the heart of Haryana in the rohtak city. Founded by eminent dental surgeon, Dr. Hrishikesh Tehri, the dental clinic has been conceptualised to deliver world class dental care to patients with care, compassion, commitment. The clinic has a state-of-the art infrastructure, stringent quality control and follows international standard protocols.

The clinic has a highly experienced team of dentist in Haryana who follow interdisciplinary approach with cutting edge techniques for the benefit of their patients. A one-of-its-kind facility aspiring to create an ethical & safe environment to treat and more importantly prevent dental diseases. We are the proud recipient of Famdent “Excellence in Dentistry Awards” for 2 consecutive years. No wonder we are rated as the Best dental clinic in Haryana.

The clinic is designed to give you a pleasant and calming environment. The concept works at all sensory levels – visual, sound, smells, taste and touch and is effective in bringing down anxiety levels in patients. Our operatory rooms have low pristine white walls combined with glass sections to give a feeling of openness, each room is equipped with soothing music, and the subtle Jasmine fragrance gives you a therapeutic feel to complete the experience.

Today, while stress and anxiety levels are on the rise, so is the need to move towards a lifestyle which brings positive energy and wellness in our daily routine. Smayate was incepted with this singular goal to “bring smile” to people’s lives. The name Smayate has its roots in the Sanskrit word which means to “smile” or “shine”. Our endeavor is to achieve just this by leaving a smile on your face and a shine on your teeth!!

At Tehri Dental Clinic we offer timings to fit your busy lifestyle. We are open from Monday through Saturday and from 9 AM to 2 PM and from 4:30 PM to 7PM. We offer convenient payment options for our patients. We facilitate easy treatment payment transactions via all leading credit cards (visa, mastercard), cheques, drafts, and online transfer.
Payment can also be made through easy interest free installments.

Our priority is to take our patients through merits of good dental habits and offer best service and treatment. We understand all of us have a deep lying fear of a visit to a dental surgeon which often brings back childhood memories of a visit to a traditional old fashioned dental clinic; We at Tehri Dental want to break the old mold and give you an environment and experience that matches your expectations.
